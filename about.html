About us

Built by a Depop seller who wanted a cheaper alternative.

I’m Maggiemadethis, a Depop seller who got tired of paying monthly for other renewal services. GhostPop came from wanting to offer a simpler, lower-cost option for sellers who still need help keeping their listings fresh.

Why I made GhostPop

I wanted a tool shaped by the day-to-day reality of selling on Depop. The existing services solved a real problem, but the monthly cost kept adding up, so I decided to build a more affordable version that still feels easy to use.

What GhostPop stands for

GhostPop is meant to be practical, approachable, and seller-focused. The goal is to make listing renewal more manageable without overcomplicating the workflow or pricing it like another heavy subscription product.

From seller to builder

Made for people actively running a shop

GhostPop was built from the perspective of someone actually managing listings, staying active, and trying to grow a shop efficiently. It exists because I wanted to provide a cheaper option that still solves the same repetitive problem.
